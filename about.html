Rohun Dhar is a sophomore at Tufts University currently studying Computer Science and Japanese. He can be found on campus in the music hall in a practice room or in the computer science lab; You won't have to look further than those two locations guaranteed. Having only built a modest number of programs so far, he is looking forward to using his sophomore year to gain the skills necessary to engage in opportunities in real world programming in the summer of 2016, when he has been asked to intern at a Japanese technology company. An avid musician and composer, Rohun spent the bulk of his senior year in highs school gathering the research, team, and finances to produce a musical street-play in India to spread public health messaging about domestic violence, sexual assault, and teen suicide in the form of song and dance. Continuing to cultivate a passion for music, he will act as the Musical Conductor for both Fall and Spring Musical Productions put on at Tufts University.
